Cheesecake Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for cheesecake that I found on . It is called the "Our Best Cheesecake" and below you'll find the ingredients as well as the step-by-step direction to make your cheesecake.

Ingredients

  • Graham Cracker Crumbs (1.75 cups)
  • Butter (1/3 cup, melted)
  • Sugar (1.25 cups)
    • 1/4 cup for crust
    • 1 cup for the filling
  • Cream Cheese (24 ounces)
  • Sour Cream (1 cup)
  • Vanilla (2 teaspoons)
  • 3 Eggs
  • Pie Filling (21 ounce, OPTIONAL)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Mix graham crumbs, butter, and 1/4 cup sugar.
  3. Press crumbs onto bottom of 9-inch springform pan.
  4. Beat cream cheese and remaining sugar in large bowl with mixer until blended. Add sour cream and vanilla; mix well.
  5. Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating on low speed after each addition just until blended.
  6. Pour mixture over crust.
  7. Bake 1 hour to 1 hour 10 minutes, or until center is almost set. Run knife around rim of pan to loosen cake; cool before removing rim. Refrigerate cheesecake 4 hours.
  8. (OPTIONAL) Top with pie filling before serving.
