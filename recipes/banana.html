Banana Smoothie Recipe

Description

This is a very simple recipe for a banana smoothie. This recipe is slightly different from the rest because it is my own personal recipe. It's a very simple recipe that is quick too make and that only requires a handful of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 Bananas
  • Milk or Water (1 cup)
    • Taste better with milk but you can substitute with water for a slightly healthier drink.
  • Ice (1 cup)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Peel all three banana's and place into a blender.
  2. Add 1 cup of water or milk into blender.
  3. Add 1 cup of ice into blender.
  4. Blend for 20-30 seconds.

Optional

You can change this smoothie into any type of smoothie by simply replacing the banana for something else like mangoes, strawberries, peaches, etc.

You can also just remove 1 banana and add half a cup of strawberry to make it into a strawberry-banana smoothie.
